Stress Management Tips

Managing stress doesn’t have to be complicated. Here are some practical, science-backed tips:

  • Practice Breathing Exercises: Inhale deeply, hold, and exhale slowly to calm your body instantly.
  • Manage Your Time: Break tasks into smaller steps and prioritize what matters most.
  • Live a Healthy Lifestyle: Eat balanced meals, sleep well, and exercise regularly.
  • Practice Mindfulness: Spend a few minutes meditating or staying present each day.
  • Stay Positive: Replace negative thoughts with hopeful ones and stay motivated.
